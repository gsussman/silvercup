ABOUT

Silver Cup Capital is a real estate management firm focused on core-plus, value-added and opportunistic real estate investment strategies. The firm believes that strong investor returns come from united teams and partnerships unified behind common principles, and seeks to align people, capital, knowledge, incentives and support systems accordingly.

Founded in 2015 by individuals with significant experience in private equity, real estate and complex transnational business transactions, Silver Cup Capital focuses on select property types and specific geographic regions within its “cluster” framework and with a particular focus on creating long-term value for investors.

Silver Cup Capital’s investment philosophy is based on 3 core concepts: (i) Belief in Market Fundamentals; (ii) Dedication to Process and (iii) Flexibility and Diversity with Respect to Geography and Asset Types. These core concepts that comprise our investment philosophy are enhanced by certain business principles: (i) Reliance on Fundamentals, (ii) Systematic Research Approach, (iii) Aligning Interests, (iv) Investment Diversification and (v) Working Closely with Our Investors.

The principals of Silver Cup Capital make substantial personal investments in the operations of the Firm and alongside investors.
