BUSINESS PRINCIPLES

Silver Cup Capital has adopted certain principles to inform and enhance our Investment Philosophy.

Reliance on Fundamentals

Silver Cup Capital seeks to produce consistent and stable returns by focusing on proven strategies and relying on fundamental economic values. This includes looking beyond current supply and demand for housing to identify geographies where populations appear to be growing, examination of crime and employment data and a disciplined investigation of the specific economic situation of each property.


Systematic Research Approach

We seek to apply established research systems to each investment, verifying available information to the extent possible and working backwards from a reasonable potential cashflow to discipline investment.


Working Closely with Our Investors

Silver Cup Capital understands that every investor is unique, and that their risk appetite and investment objectives need personal, tailored support. In order to effectively meet the needs of a diverse investor base, Silver Cup Capital offers a range of investment vehicles, property types and features, investment locations, property management options, and growth profiles (such as rental revenue-driven, asset appreciation, and leveraged investment strategies). Silver Cup Capital’s diversified approach and granular opportunities for investor input and customization enables Silver Cup Capital to better address investor needs and desires and to work with a wide range of real estate investors from experienced individual and institutional investors to first-time investors.


Aligning Interests

We seek to ensure that our interests are aligned with those of our investors. To do this, we seek to deal fairly with investors and maximize their returns. Part of seeking to fully align our interests with those of our investors is focusing on deal participation, rather than management fees. The principals of Silver Cup Capital make substantial personal investments in the operations of the Firm and alongside investors. In addition to the Silver Cup Capital principals, we seek to engage proven high-quality operating partners and property managers and similarly align their interests.


Investment Diversification

Using our cluster approach, Silver Cup Capital seeks to invest between and among high potential regions, markets and areas. Additionally, Silver Cup Capital seeks to invest opportunistically, across asset types, including single family residences, small multi-family residences, small- and mid-sized apartment complexes.
